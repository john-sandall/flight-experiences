Our Top Pick

Tiger Moth Classic Experience

£285

de Havilland Tiger Moth (1930s) · 30 minutes

The quintessential vintage flight. An open-cockpit biplane experience complete with leather flying helmet and goggles. You'll feel the wind in your face as you soar over the Kent countryside in one of aviation's most beloved aircraft. Optional aerobatics available, and you can even take the controls.

Why we love it

  • Iconic open-cockpit biplane experience
  • Leather helmet & goggles provided
  • Aerobatics available if you're feeling brave
  • You can take the controls
  • Departs from historic WW2 airfield

Things to consider

  • 75 minute drive from Brighton
  • Seasonal: April to October only
  • Open cockpit means weather dependent

Castles & NT Properties Tour

£299

RAF Bulldog (1974) · 60 minutes

The most flying time for your money. A full hour in a classic military trainer, flying over Kent's most stunning stately homes — Chartwell (Churchill's home), Hever Castle, Penshurst Place, and Leeds Castle. At just £4.98 per minute, this offers exceptional value with aerobatics included.

Why we love it

  • 60 minutes of flight time — double most others
  • Best cost per minute (£4.98/min)
  • See 4 historic castles and estates
  • Aerobatics included
  • You can take the controls
  • Available year-round

Things to consider

  • 45 minute drive from Brighton
  • Military trainer (not a biplane)
  • Enclosed cockpit

Nearest to Home

South Downs Discovery

£175

Piper Super Cub (1954) · 30 minutes

The closest and most convenient option. Just 20 minutes from Brighton, this flight takes you over the stunning Seven Sisters cliffs, Beachy Head, and the South Downs National Park. A gentle, scenic flight in a classic 1950s taildragger aircraft — perfect if aerobatics aren't your thing.

Why we love it

  • Only 20 minutes from Brighton!
  • Beautiful coastal scenery
  • Most affordable option
  • You can take the controls
  • Classic 1950s aircraft

Things to consider

  • No aerobatics available
  • Seasonal: March to October
  • Shorter flight time

Aviation Heritage

Chipmunk Military Experience

£225

de Havilland Chipmunk (1950s) · 45 minutes

Fly the aircraft that trained RAF pilots. The Chipmunk was the primary trainer for the RAF for decades — this is the exact type of aircraft used to train everyone from fighter pilots to Prince Charles. Includes views of the White Cliffs of Dover, Canterbury Cathedral, and historic RAF Manston.

Why we love it

  • 45 minutes of flight time
  • Genuine RAF training aircraft
  • Good value at £5.00/min
  • Aerobatics available
  • You can take the controls
  • Available year-round

Things to consider

  • 75 minute drive from Brighton
  • Not an open-cockpit aircraft
